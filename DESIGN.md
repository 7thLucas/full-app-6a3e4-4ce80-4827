# Design Guidelines for PropLedger

## Color Palette
- Primary: Dark navy (#1B2B4B)
- Accent: Forest green (#2D6A4F)
- Background: Light grey (#F5F7FA)
- Surface: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Dark (#1A1A2E), Muted (#6B7280)
- Positive (profit): Green (#22C55E)
- Negative (loss): Red (#EF4444)

## Typography
- Font: Inter or system sans-serif
- Headings: Bold, dark navy
- Data/numbers: Monospace or tabular figures for financial data

## Components
- Expense entry: Prominent, always-accessible input form (top of screen or floating action)
- Unit search: Autocomplete/typeahead input — instant filtering
- P&L cards: Property cards showing net monthly result with color-coded profit/loss
- Tables: Clean, bordered, sortable for expense lists

## Principles
- Speed first: the expense entry flow should be 3 taps/clicks max
- Financial clarity: numbers are big, color-coded, and unambiguous
- Mobile-friendly: managers may log expenses on-site
- No clutter: show what matters, hide what doesn't
