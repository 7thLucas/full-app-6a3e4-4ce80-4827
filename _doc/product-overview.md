# Property P&L Tracker — Product Overview

## What the product is
A monthly property profit-and-loss tracking app for property owners and real estate investors. Users log income and expenses for each property each month; the app automatically calculates and displays a clear profit/loss result per property, per month, with a portfolio-wide summary dashboard.

## Users
- **Primary**: Individual property owners and landlords managing 2–20 rental or investment properties
- **Secondary**: Small property management operators tracking owner portfolios

## Problem being solved
Managing financials across multiple properties is fragmented. Rent income lands in one account; maintenance bills arrive by email; mortgage, insurance, taxes, and management fees are tracked in different places — or not at all. At month-end, most owners have no quick way to see which properties made money and which didn't. Piecing it together manually in spreadsheets is slow, error-prone, and easy to abandon.

## Solution
A centralized monthly data-entry and reporting tool: log income and expenses by category for each property each month. The app instantly calculates profit or loss per property, shows trends over time, and provides a portfolio-wide snapshot — so the owner always knows where they stand without needing an accounting background.

## Core features
1. **Property Portfolio Management** — Add, name, and organise multiple properties in one place (name, type, address)
2. **Monthly Income Entry** — Log rent, fees, and other income by month per property in a structured form
3. **Expense Tracking** — Record mortgage, maintenance, insurance, utilities, management fees, taxes, and other costs by category per property per month
4. **Automatic P&L Calculation** — Net profit/loss computed automatically per property per month — no formulas to manage
5. **Portfolio Dashboard** — A single-screen view of all properties with monthly and year-to-date performance at a glance
6. **Year-to-Date Summaries** — Running annual totals to track cumulative performance and simplify tax preparation

## Strategic principles
- **Simplicity first**: No double-entry bookkeeping, no complex chart-of-accounts setup — monthly income/expense entry is all that's required
- **Monthly cadence by design**: The product is built around the natural monthly billing and payment cycle of property ownership
- **Multi-property by default**: The portfolio overview is the home screen, not an afterthought; every metric surfaces per property and in aggregate
- **No accounting degree required**: Language is owner-facing ("profit this month", "what you spent on maintenance") rather than accounting-technical

## Operational model
- Single user (property owner) entering data manually each month
- Data is entered once per property per month
- Reports are available immediately after entry
- Primary sessions are short (~5–15 min/month per property)
