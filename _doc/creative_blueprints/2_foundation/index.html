Why it exists
⚠️ The Problem

Property financials are scattered everywhere.

Rent lands in one account. Maintenance bills arrive by email. Mortgage, insurance, taxes, and management fees live in different places — or nowhere at all. At month-end, most owners have no quick answer to the only question that matters: did this property make money?

✅ The Solution

One place. One monthly view. Clear P&L per property.

Log income and expenses for each property each month. Profit or loss is calculated automatically — and surfaced across your entire portfolio in a single dashboard. No accounting background needed.
