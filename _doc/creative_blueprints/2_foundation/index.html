Why it exists
⚠️ The Problem

Allocating expenses across 500+ units is a slow, painful scroll.

Every bill needs to land on the right unit. But when you have 500+ units in a list, finding the correct one takes longer than the entry itself. Multiply that by every expense every month, and you're losing hours to pure friction — before you even get to the P&L.

✅ The Solution

Type amount + unit. Done. P&L calculated.

Instant unit search surfaces the right unit in a keystroke — no scrolling. Enter the amount, the expense is allocated. Revenue comes from your accountant; the app handles the rest and produces a clear monthly profit/loss per unit automatically.
